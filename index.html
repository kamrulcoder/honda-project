HONDA BIKE PRICE IN BD, 2021

Honda- the power of dream, Yes Bangladeshi peoples are also dreaming by the power of Honda. Honda has started manufacturing their motorcycle products in Bangladesh with BHL (Bangladesh Honda Private Limited) . In the near future Bangladesh motorcycle industry will be rising greatly followed by Honda. As a result motorcycle will be fair-priced in BD Market. Bangladesh Honda Private Limited (BHL) operates motorcycle industry segment in Bangladesh under a joint venture company between Honda Motor Company Limited (Japan), and Bangladesh Steel and Engineering Corporation under The Ministry of Industry, (Bangladesh). The company started in Bangladesh as a private limited company on 04-Dec-2012, and joint project agreement was signed on 27-Sep-2012 .
